FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our services, support, and products.

How can I contact support?

If you need assistance or have any questions, you can reach our support team by phone or email. Our dedicated team is here to help you with any issues or inquiries you may have.

What are your service offerings?

We offer a wide range of services including security integration, card access systems, CCTV camera systems, and more. Our team of experts can design, install, and service high-quality security solutions for any facility.

How can I inquire about your products?

If you have any inquiries about our products, you can contact our sales team. They will be happy to provide you with more information and assist you with your product-related questions.

Can you assist with large building projects?

Yes, we specialize in working with large building contractors and electricians to supply and incorporate high security technologies into large building projects. Our team has the expertise to design and install intelligent access control systems for any facility.

What brands do you work with?

We work with top brands such as KeyScan, Aurora, dormakaba, Avigilon, Mobotix, Pelco, and Sensormatic. Our team is trained to install and service the latest closed circuit camera systems and integrated camera systems.
